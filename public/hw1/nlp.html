What is Natural Language Processing?
Natural language processing is the process of writing a program which is capable of understanding human language, in text or speech form. These programs have a wide variety of uses, such as machine translation, document analysis, web scraping, etc. The process begins, like all other machine learning, with training data. A sentence could be fed to a model, and from it the sentence would be segmented, tokenized, lematized, and then the information can be extracted. NLP is one of the oldest forms of machine learning, and in fact was what kicked off the now popular neural net structure.
